Why Does Fitness Matter?

Physical fitness is crucial for maintaining overall health, preventing chronic diseases (such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, and certain cancers), and enhancing mental well-being. Regular exercise, ideally 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly, boosts energy, improves sleep quality, strengthens muscles/bones, and reduces anxiety or depression.
